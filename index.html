Experience

Interning at Ann Shippy, MD

During the Summer of 2022, I interned for a Functional Medicine Doctor, who used an outdated, rudimentary HTML structure in what she called her patients' nutritional recommendations page; a database program, where each patient's individualized notes, list of test kits, and supplements as recommended by the doctor were saved into a private server system, following HIPPA Law regulations.

When the office manager first introduced me to how to edit this program—which had been previously modified by employees with no true coding experience—I was overwhelmed by its lack of organization, structure, and the amount of basic syntactical errors that made me question how the code was even able to function as intended. With my basic background in HTML and coding knowledge from past semesters, I offered to take on the project of debugging and nearly rewriting the entire system to be more user-friendly for the other employees to be able to continue updating in the future. I even consulted with the doctor personally, in order to include any changes that she was hoping to implement into the system and by the end of the summer, I'd done all that they has asked of me, and left with a better sense of understanding in terms of working with HTML, private server systems, and how to collaborate effectively and beneficially in a team environment.

In addition to this project, I also aided in creating simple graphics and posts for the Doctor's social media pages, including Instagram and Pinterest, as well as for the supplement shop on her website.

During my second summer interning for Dr. Shippy (2023), I designed compelling product images and infographics for the e-commerce site, emphasizing product benefits to prospective customers, and revamped the online supplement shop.
